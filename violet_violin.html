About Us

Violet Violin Accessibility is an accessibility-focused startup founded by Matthew J. Damiano. Our mission is to design assistive software and hardware that empower individuals with speech, mobility, and sensory disabilities to communicate and create freely.

Our Focus

Inclusive Design

Building accessible interfaces from the ground up — not as an afterthought.

Adaptive Software

We specialize in AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) tools and custom Python solutions that respond to the user’s needs.

Community Empowerment

Working alongside educators, therapists, and creators to improve accessibility standards.

Featured Project: ORBY AAC

ORBY AAC is a speech-generation application designed to be intuitive, fast, and highly customizable. It combines speech synthesis, visual categories, and a clean interface to make communication more natural.
